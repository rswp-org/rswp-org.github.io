how we work

🤝 local organisations

Our partnerships are the heart of who we are and how we work. We don’t believe in helicoptering in to provide “aid” – we think that local organisations understand best what their communities need. So we work with them and help with funding, operational support, and long term learning.

🔁 sustainable services

Sustainability is obviously a bit of a buzzword at the moment, but for water services it’s vital! For us, sustainability means water services need to be three key things – reliable, affordable, and well-managed – as well as environmentally efficient.

📈 data and evidence

Data is how we keep ourselves accountable – whether that’s to our donors, our partners, or the schools we serve. We use it to understand what’s needed, be clear on our impact, and learn how we can do better next time.
